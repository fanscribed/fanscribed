Please use these guidelines while transcribing:

Start a new line when you hear:
  • A different speaker.
  • A new sentence by the same speaker.
Type what you hear, with these exceptions:
  • Leave out partial words at the start and end of the sound clip.
  • Leave out "crutch words": um, ah, like, you know
  • Leave out repeated words, stuttering, and stammering.
  • Use "going to" instead of "gonna", etc.
Capitalize the following:
  • "I".
  • Proper names.
  • Clear beginning of a new sentence.
Don't include the following — it's handled later:
  • Punctuation you're not sure of.
  • Speaker identification.
Don't fret about punctuation or capitalization. It will be cleaned up during later sentence-cleaning tasks.
Use the following shorthand:
  • [word?] - audible but difficult speech; unknown spelling
  • [?] - audible but unknown speech
  • [i] - inaudible speech
  • :) - laughter
  • [m] - music
  • [j] - jingles
  • [sfx] - sound effects
  • ... - trailing off; significant pause
  • -- - interrupted sentence
  • - - inserting new thought in mid-sentence
